About me

I began my journey into the world of programming by tinkering with video game mods. A relatively simple papryus scripting system kept me busy, but not enthralled. My realization of my passion for computer science and programming came alongside an introductory computer science course during my first semester of my freshman year in college. Since then, I have come to realize both the power and limitations of computers. Data structures and algorithms in C++ put into scope the application of computer programming to problem-solving and theory, while numerical computing techniques and MatLab shed light on the shortcomings and mathematical applicatons of these powerful, but flawed machines. With that said, I have found computer science and programming to be completely compatible with my desire to tinker, to theorize, and to solve.
